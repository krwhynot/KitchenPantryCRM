# Product Requirements Document: PantryCRM

## Project Overview
- **Name:** PantryCRM (Kitchen Pantry Customer Relationship Management System)
- **Description:** A Laravel 10.x application with Filament PHP v3.3+ for managing food service business relationships, including organizations, contacts, interactions, and sales pipeline management.
- **Technology Stack:** Laravel 10.x, Filament PHP v3.3+, SQLite database, Azure App Service deployment.
- **Budget Constraint:** $18/month hosting budget.
- **Performance Requirements:** Sub-second search, 2-second page loads, 10-second report generation.
